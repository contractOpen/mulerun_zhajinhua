# 骡子炸金花 零预算营销执行计划

> 版本：v2.0 | 日期：2026-03-22 | 状态：待执行
> 核心原则：**零现金投入**，所有营销手段依靠免费渠道、社区自增长和游戏内积分激励

## 一、产品定位

- **品类**：Web3 社交棋牌游戏（炸金花/三张牌）
- **核心卖点**：即开即玩、链上透明、多链支持、Telegram 原生 Mini App
- **技术优势**：智能合约保证公平性，零下载门槛，跨链资产互通
- **目标**：成为 Telegram 生态头部炸金花游戏，6 个月内 DAU 突破 10,000
- **营销哲学**：初始没有钱，营销应该使用免费的。通过产品力和社区裂变驱动增长，不花一分钱广告费

## 二、目标用户画像

| 用户类型 | 特征 | 获取渠道（全部免费） | 转化策略 |
|---------|------|-------------------|---------|
| Crypto Degens | 熟悉链上操作，追求高回报，活跃于 Twitter/TG | Twitter 自然话题传播、免费 Alpha 群合作、社区互推 | 链上透明牌局 + 积分奖励 + 排行榜竞技 |
| TG 游戏玩家 | 已习惯 TG Mini App，偏好轻量休闲游戏 | TG 游戏群互推、Bot 裂变分享、群内口碑传播 | 零门槛体验 + 每日签到 + 好友对战 |
| 休闲棋牌玩家 | 喜欢传统炸金花，社交驱动 | 免费社群导流、用户自发邀请、UGC 内容吸引 | 熟悉玩法 + 积分体系 + 锦标赛 |

- [ ] 整理免费合作清单：至少 20 个潜在互推伙伴（TG 游戏群、crypto 社区、TON 生态项目）

## 四、冷启动阶段（第 3-4 周）

### 4.1 种子用户招募（目标：500 人）
1. 创始团队各自邀请 20+ 好友体验，首批 100 人（Day 1-2）
2. 在 10+ 个免费 crypto/游戏 TG 群发布体验邀请（Day 1-5，注意遵守群规，以分享体验而非广告的方式）
3. 官方 TG 群每日免费 AMA，解答玩法问题（Day 1-14）
4. 在 Twitter/X 创建 #MulePoker 话题，团队成员每日发推（Day 1 起持续）
5. 主动联系 5+ 个小型 TG 游戏/crypto 社区，提议免费互推

### 4.2 每日奖励引导（纯积分，零成本）
- 新用户注册送 500 积分
- 每日登录奖励：3 次 x 500 积分/天
- 首次完成对局额外奖励 1,000 积分
- 所有奖励均为游戏内积分，无任何法币支出

### 4.3 好友对战裂变活动（纯积分激励）
- 邀请 3 位好友注册并完成 1 局 -> 邀请人获 2,000 积分
- 被邀请人同时获得 500 积分加成
- 邀请排行榜：周 Top 10 额外奖励 5,000 积分
- 设计"邀请进度条"视觉效果，激发用户完成目标

### 4.4 Twitter/X 自然传播活动
- 发布 #MulePoker 话题活动
- 参与方式：发推分享游戏体验 + 关注官方 + 加入 TG 群 = 获得积分奖励
- 每周在 TG 群公布"最佳推文"，制造持续话题
- 团队成员用个人账号积极互动、评论、引用转发

**执行模板**：每项任务指定负责人、截止日期、验收标准，通过免费项目管理工具（Notion/Trello 免费版）跟踪。

## 五、增长阶段（第 5-8 周）

### 5.2 免费内容营销

- 团队成员在 Reddit、Bitcointalk 等免费论坛发布项目介绍

- 中文群：核心团队运营，每日活跃互动

- 设立"社区大使"计划：活跃用户自愿担任推广角色，获得积分和专属头衔

### 5.4 游戏内活动（仅积分成本）
- 排行榜上线：按周/月统计胜率和积分
- 周赛：每周六晚 8 点锦标赛，奖池 50,000 积分（游戏内生成，零成本）
- 月赛：月末大型锦标赛，奖池 200,000 积分 + 专属头像框
- 新手保护赛：低门槛锦标赛，帮助新用户融入

### 5.5 推荐奖励系统（纯积分，零成本）
- 永久绑定推荐关系
- 被推荐人每次对局，推荐人获 5% 积分返利
- 推荐达 10 人解锁 VIP 特权（专属牌桌皮肤、头像框）
- 推荐达 50 人解锁"推广大使"称号和额外积分加成
- 生成个人专属邀请链接和邀请海报（Bot 自动生成）

### 5.6 用户生成内容（UGC）激励
- 鼓励玩家分享战绩卡片到社交媒体
- 每周评选"最佳战绩分享"，奖励积分
- 举办"牌局故事征文"活动，优质内容获得积分 + 群内展示
- 建立玩家攻略合集，由社区贡献内容

- **自然变现**：当用户基数足够大后，通过游戏内积分消耗（高级牌桌入场费）和未来代币经济实现变现

## 十、骡子 AI Bot 推广计划

- **定位**：AI Bot 作为核心免费增长引擎和用户服务入口，替代所有付费推广渠道的作用
- **功能模块**：
  - 自动欢迎新用户，引导完成注册和首局（减少人工运营成本）
  - 游戏教学：炸金花规则、牌型大小、策略建议（替代付费内容制作）
  - 智能客服：常见问题自动回复，7x24 无人工成本
  - 对战匹配：通过 Bot 快速开局，邀请好友（降低使用门槛）
- **群内集成**：作为官方 TG 群助手，维护秩序 + 活跃氛围（替代付费社区运营）
- **病毒传播机制（核心免费增长引擎）**：
  - **分享得积分**：用户将 Bot 分享给好友，双方各获 500 积分
  - **战绩卡片**：Bot 自动生成精美对战战绩卡片（含游戏链接），用户自发分享到 Twitter/TG/社交媒体
  - **群内播报**：自动播报精彩牌局（皇家同花顺、大逆转等），激发围观和参与
  - **每日挑战**：Bot 推送每日挑战任务（如"今日赢 3 局"），完成获积分，分享挑战进度到群内
  - **排行榜推送**：Bot 定时推送群内/全服排行榜变动，制造竞争氛围
  - **邀请海报生成**：Bot 为每个用户生成个人专属邀请海报（含邀请码），一键分享
- **增长飞轮**：好玩的游戏 -> 用户自发分享战绩 -> 新用户被吸引加入 -> 更多对局 -> 更多精彩内容 -> 更多分享（零成本正循环）

# MuleRun Poker (Zha Jin Hua) Zero-Budget Marketing Execution Plan

> Version: v2.0 | Date: 2026-03-22 | Status: Pending Execution
> Core Principle: **Zero cash investment**. All marketing relies on free channels, organic community growth, and in-game point incentives.

## 1. Product Positioning

- **Category**: Web3 social card game (Zha Jin Hua / 3-Card Poker)
- **Core selling points**: Instant play, on-chain transparency, multi-chain support, Telegram-native Mini App
- **Technical edge**: Smart contracts ensure fairness, zero-download, cross-chain asset interoperability
- **Goal**: Become the #1 Zha Jin Hua game in Telegram ecosystem, DAU > 10,000 within 6 months
- **Marketing philosophy**: No initial budget -- marketing must be free. Growth driven by product quality and community virality, zero ad spend.

## 2. Target User Profiles

| Segment | Traits | Acquisition Channel (All Free) | Conversion Strategy |
|---------|--------|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| Crypto Degens | On-chain savvy, high-risk appetite, active on Twitter/TG | Organic Twitter threads, free alpha group partnerships, community cross-promo | On-chain transparent games + point rewards + leaderboard |
| TG Gamers | Familiar with TG Mini Apps, prefer lightweight casual games | TG game group cross-promo, Bot viral sharing, word-of-mouth | Zero-barrier entry + daily check-in + friend battles |
| Casual Card Players | Enjoy traditional card games, socially driven | Free community referrals, user invites, UGC content attraction | Familiar gameplay + points system + tournaments |

## 3. Pre-Launch Preparation (Weeks 1-2)

- [ ] Deploy TE (test environment) with full regression testing
- [ ] Deploy PE (production environment) with monitoring, logging, and alerts
- [ ] Create TG Bot and configure Mini App (including inline mode)
- [ ] Deploy smart contracts (TON testnet first, then mainnet after verification)
- [ ] Set up official TG groups (Chinese + English, each with admin Bot)
- [ ] Team-made promotional assets: 10 screenshots, 5 GIFs, 3 short videos (15s) -- using Canva free tier + screen recording
- [ ] Compile free partnership list: 20+ potential cross-promo partners (TG game groups, crypto communities, TON ecosystem projects)
- [ ] Launch landing page (game intro, rules, one-click entry -- free hosting)
- [ ] Legal compliance review (gambling regulations per target market)
- [ ] Prepare community AMA scripts and FAQ documents

**Owner**: PM leads coordination; Tech lead handles deployment; entire team participates in asset creation and cold-start seeding.

## 4. Cold Start Phase (Weeks 3-4)

### 4.1 Seed User Recruitment (Target: 500)
1. Each founding team member personally invites 20+ friends, first 100 users (Day 1-2)
2. Post experience invites in 10+ free crypto/gaming TG groups (Day 1-5, share as genuine experience not spam)
3. Daily free AMA in official TG group (Day 1-14)
4. Create #MulePoker hashtag on Twitter/X, team members post daily (Day 1 onward)
5. Proactively reach out to 5+ small TG game/crypto communities for free cross-promotion

### 4.2 Daily Reward Incentives (Points Only, Zero Cost)
- New user signup bonus: 500 points
- Daily login: 3x 500 points/day
- First completed game bonus: 1,000 points
- All rewards are in-game points -- zero fiat currency expenditure

### 4.3 Friend Battle Viral Campaign (Points-Only Incentives)
- Invite 3 friends who each complete 1 game -> inviter gets 2,000 points
- Invitees also receive 500 bonus points
- Weekly invite leaderboard: top 10 get extra 5,000 points
- Design "invite progress bar" visual to motivate users to hit milestones

### 4.4 Twitter/X Organic Campaign
- Launch #MulePoker hashtag campaign
- Participation: post about game experience + follow official account + join TG group = earn points
- Weekly "best tweet" selection announced in TG group for sustained engagement
- Team members use personal accounts to actively engage, comment, and retweet

**Execution template**: Each task assigned an owner, deadline, and acceptance criteria; tracked via free project management tools (Notion/Trello free tier).

## 5. Growth Phase (Weeks 5-8)

### 5.1 Community Cross-Promotion Partnerships (Zero Cost)
- Find 10+ similarly-sized TG game/crypto projects for cross-promotion
- Formats: mutual pinned recommendations, joint events, banner swaps
- Priority: TON ecosystem projects (maximum shared interest)
- Join TON ecosystem project directories and alliances for free exposure

### 5.2 Free Content Marketing
- Daily high-quality Twitter/X content (poker strategy, hand replay highlights, player stories)
- Create thread series: "Zha Jin Hua Winning Strategies", "Web3 Gaming Trends" -- educational content
- Record free tutorial videos, publish on Twitter and YouTube
- Participate in crypto/gaming Twitter Spaces (free) to share the project
- Team members post project introductions on Reddit, Bitcointalk, and other free forums

### 5.3 Community Self-Governance (Zero Staffing Cost)
- Chinese group: core team operates, daily engagement
- English group: recruit volunteer moderators (rewarded with in-game points + exclusive VIP status)
- Russian group: similarly recruit enthusiastic users as moderators with point incentives
- Establish "Community Ambassador" program: active users voluntarily take on promotion roles, earning points and exclusive titles

### 5.4 In-Game Events (Points Cost Only)
- Leaderboard launch: weekly/monthly win rate and points rankings
- Weekly tournament: every Saturday 8 PM, prize pool 50,000 points (system-generated, zero cost)
- Monthly championship: end-of-month major tournament, prize pool 200,000 points + exclusive avatar frame
- Beginner-friendly tournament: low entry barrier to help new users integrate

### 5.5 Referral Reward System (Points Only, Zero Cost)
- Permanent referral binding
- Referrer earns 5% point rebate on every game played by referee
- Refer 10 users to unlock VIP privileges (exclusive table skins, avatar frames)
- Refer 50 users to unlock "Promotion Ambassador" title with bonus point multipliers
- Bot auto-generates personalized invite links and invite posters

### 5.6 User-Generated Content (UGC) Incentives
- Encourage players to share battle result cards on social media
- Weekly "best result share" selection, rewarded with points
- "Hand story" writing contests -- quality content earns points + in-group showcase
- Build community-contributed strategy collection

## 6. Retention and Monetization (Ongoing)

- **Daily check-in** (implemented): escalating rewards over 7-day cycle, points only
- **New game modes**: Texas Hold'em, Dou Di Zhu, etc. -- 1 new mode every 2 months (dev cost absorbed by existing team workload)
- **VIP tier system**: 5 levels with exclusive tables, avatar frames, emote packs (virtual assets, no extra cost)
- **Seasonal events**: Lunar New Year red envelope games, Christmas specials, anniversary (prize pools are in-game points)
- **Data-driven**: User behavior analytics, A/B testing key flows (using free tools like PostHog open-source, Amplitude free tier)
- **Community-driven retention**: Regular in-group mini-events (card guessing, trivia) to boost engagement and belonging
- **Organic monetization**: Once user base is large enough, monetize via in-game point sinks (premium table entry fees) and future token economy

## 9. Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Smart contract vulnerability | Medium | High | Multiple rounds of internal review before launch; use open-source audit tools; implement pause mechanism |
| Slow user growth (no paid channels) | High | High | Increase viral incentives; expand cross-promo partnerships; optimize invite funnel |
| Regulatory risk (gambling) | Medium | High | Points-based system (not direct cash); monitor regulatory changes continuously |
| Poor cross-promo results | Medium | Medium | Test multiple channels in parallel; quickly drop low-performing partners; focus on high-conversion ones |
| Server overload | Low | High | Early user volume is manageable; leverage free/low-cost cloud elastic scaling |
| Competitor imitation | Medium | Medium | Rapid product iteration; build deep community relationships as moat |
| Volunteer moderator churn | Medium | Medium | Build moderator promotion ladder; escalating point rewards; foster pride and ownership |
| Insufficient content output | Medium | Medium | Maintain content calendar; templatize production; incentivize UGC |

## 10. Mule AI Bot Growth Plan

- **Positioning**: AI Bot as the core free growth engine and user service entry point, replacing the role of all paid marketing channels
- **Feature modules**:
  - Auto-welcome new users; guide registration and first game (eliminates manual ops cost)
  - Game tutorials: Zha Jin Hua rules, hand rankings, strategy tips (replaces paid content creation)
  - Smart customer service: auto-reply FAQs, 24/7 with zero labor cost
  - Match-making: quick game start via Bot, invite friends (lowers barrier to play)
- **Group integration**: Official TG group assistant for moderation + engagement (replaces paid community management)
- **Viral mechanics (core free growth engine)**:
  - **Share for points**: Users share the Bot with friends; both parties earn 500 points
  - **Battle result cards**: Bot auto-generates polished battle result cards (with game link); users organically share to Twitter/TG/social media
  - **In-group broadcasts**: Auto-broadcast highlight hands (royal flush, dramatic comebacks) to spark spectating and participation
  - **Daily challenges**: Bot pushes daily challenge tasks (e.g., "Win 3 games today"); completion earns points; challenge progress shared in group
  - **Leaderboard pushes**: Bot periodically posts group/global leaderboard changes to create competitive atmosphere
  - **Invite poster generator**: Bot creates personalized invite posters (with invite code) for each user -- one-tap share
- **Growth flywheel**: Fun game -> users organically share results -> new users attracted -> more matches -> more exciting content -> more sharing (zero-cost positive feedback loop)
